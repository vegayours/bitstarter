What is morphing?

Sound morphing is an amazing audio effect of blending a number of sounds to a new one which has characteristics of source sounds. It allows you to create potentially unlimited number of new sounds from finite source sounds. You could create unique synthetic sounds which you will never hear in real life. Have you ever listened female vocal filled with harp?

Mixing is not morphing

"Mixing" or "fading" is very well known sound effect that exists in most audio players. It gives a simple transition of one sound to another. Mixing produces simple interpolation of source sounds amplitude which yields a sound in which both source sounds could be separated. Morphing, on the contrary, yields a sound with intermediate characteristics and listens as one sound.
